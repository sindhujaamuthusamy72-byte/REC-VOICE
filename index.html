How It Works

  1. Pick a category — Teaching & Staff, or Management & Administration.
  2. Describe the issue — no names required, just what happened and where.
  3. It gets listed publicly — anyone can see it, upvote it with "Me Too", and track its status.
  4. Student council reviews it — common or serious issues get escalated to the right department.

Why Anonymous?

A lot of real problems — biased grading, favoritism, unsafe hostel conditions, unfair fee practices — never get reported because students fear being singled out. REC Voice removes that barrier entirely. There are no login fields, no name fields, and no tracking. The issue speaks for itself.

Note: this is a static practice project — submissions here are for demonstration only and are not sent to any real college department.
